Cancellation Policy

 

At Hackwit Technologies Private Limited, we offer flexible subscription plans for creators to explore and enhance their journey with AI video creation. Please review our cancellation policy for each plan below:

 

Free Plan

 

Cost: $0 per month

Features:

  1. * Generate 1 free course
  2. * 5 sub topics
  3. * AI Teacher
  4. * Theory & Image

 

Cancellation Terms:

 

  1. 1. You can cancel your Free Plan at any time without any charges.
  2. 2. No further actions are needed as no payments are involved.

 

Monthly Plan

 

Cost: $1 per month

Features:

  1. * Generate 3 Courses/Month
  2. * 10 sub topics
  3. * AI Teacher
  4. * Theory & Image
  5. * Theory & Video Course

 

Cancellation Terms:

 

  1. 1. You can cancel your Monthly Plan at any time.
  2. 2. Upon cancellation, you will retain access to your plan features until the end of your current billing cycle.
  3. 3. No refunds will be provided for the remaining days of the current billing period.

 

Monthly Pro Plan

 

Cost: $5 per month

Features:

  1. * Generate 20 Courses/Month
  2. * 10 sub topics
  3. * AI Teacher
  4. * Theory & Image
  5. * Theory & Video Course

 

Cancellation Terms:

 

  1. 1. You can cancel your Monthly Pro Plan at any time.
  2. 2. Upon cancellation, you will retain access to your plan features until the end of your current billing cycle.
  3. 3. No refunds will be provided for the remaining days of the current billing period.

 

How to Cancel:

 

  1. 1.To cancel your subscription, please log into your account, navigate to the subscription settings, and select "Cancel Subscription".
  2. 2. You will receive a confirmation email once your cancellation is processed.

For any assistance or questions regarding your subscription or the cancellation process, please contact our support team at support@pickmycourse.io.
